topics in development

What I teach.

The themes running through my courses, talks, and supervision.

Compassionate supervision

How to mentor BTs and developing analysts with clarity, warmth, and high standards — the kind of supervision we all wish we'd had.

The LISTEN Reset

A structured framework for navigating the moment a session stops working — slowing down, staying in scope, and communicating clearly.

Culturally responsive practice

Partnering with families across cultures and languages with humility, respect, and genuine collaboration at the center.

Observation vs. interpretation

Sharpening the core clinical skill of describing what you actually see — the foundation of good data and good decisions.

Ethics in everyday practice

Recognizing scope, raising concerns, and making values-aligned decisions in the messy reality of day-to-day sessions.

Building capacity internationally

Growing sustainable, well-trained behavior-analytic practice in places where the field is still taking root.
