questions

you might be wondering...

Is this only for autistic kids?+

Not at all. While I specialize in early intervention and have extensive experience with autistic children, my approach works for ALL children navigating big emotions, behavior challenges, or transitions. If your child has feelings, behaviors, and a need to be understood — this is for you.

What ages do you work with?+

My main focus is ages 0-5 (early intervention years), but the principles apply to older children too. If you're working with kids of any age and want to understand behavior on a deeper level, my content and trainings will serve you.

I'm not a parent — I'm a BCBA/BT. Is this for me?+

Yes! A huge part of my work is supporting professionals in our field who want to do this work better. I supervise BCBA candidates, train BTs, and offer CEUs for BCBAs + QABAs and PDUs for RBTs. My approach is affirming, evidence-based, and modern.

Do you work with families internationally?+

Yes! I serve families in the USA, Portugal, Brazil, and across Europe. My services are offered in English and Portuguese. Wherever you are, if my approach resonates, I'd love to connect.

What makes your approach different?+

I focus on the WHY behind behavior, not just the WHAT. Traditional approaches often prioritize compliance over connection. I prioritize understanding, autonomy, and dignity. My work is affirming, person-centered, and built on the belief that all behavior is communication.

How do I get started?+

Easy! Start with the free guide. Then follow me on Instagram for weekly content. When you're ready to work together, send me an email at hello@stephanielistens.com.
