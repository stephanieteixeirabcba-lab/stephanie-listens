questions

you might be wondering...

I'm a BT, RBT, or BCBA — what do you offer me?+

A growing library of practical resources: the free Session Reset Pocket Guide, the LISTEN Reset Kit, 1-hour CEUs launching September 2026, and 1:1 BCBA supervision. Everything is built to teach the skills your certification didn't — compassionate, evidence-based, and grounded in real sessions.

When are the CEUs available, and who are they for?+

CEUs launch September 2026. Most are 1-hour, on-demand courses for behavior analysts and technicians, approved for IBAO and QABA. Topics in development include compassionate supervision, the LISTEN Reset framework, and culturally responsive care. Get early access here.

What makes your approach different?+

I focus on the WHY behind behavior, not just the WHAT. Traditional approaches often prioritize compliance over connection. I prioritize understanding, autonomy, and dignity — and I teach practitioners how to hold clinical rigor and compassion at the same time.

Do you work with people internationally?+

Yes. I work with practitioners and families in the USA, Portugal, Brazil, and across Europe, in English and Portuguese. I've also presented internationally, including at the ABAI conference on training ABA professionals in Portugal.

Do you still work with parents?+

I do. Parent coaching is offered as a 1:1 service in English or Portuguese, focused on helping families understand the why behind behavior. Most of my work now centers on teaching and supporting ABA professionals, but families are always welcome.

How do I get started?+

Start with the free Session Reset Pocket Guide or take the practitioner style quiz. Then follow me on Instagram for weekly content. When you're ready to work together, email me at stephaniet@stephanielistens.com.
